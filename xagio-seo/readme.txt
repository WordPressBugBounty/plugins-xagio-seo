== Description ==

**Xagio SEO** is a comprehensive WordPress plugin that seamlessly combines WordPress management with advanced AI-driven Search Engine Optimization. Whether you're a seasoned SEO expert or a beginner, Xagio SEO provides you with all the tools necessary to optimize your website from a single, user-friendly interface. With this plugin, you can manage your SEO strategy, monitor performance, and apply best practices, all without leaving your WordPress dashboard.

**Key Features:**

– **AI SEO Analysis:** Identify SEO opportunities in real-time. Our AI examines on-page elements, backlink profiles, keyword density, and content structure to give you data-backed recommendations on improving ranking and visibility.
– **AI Content Generation & Optimization:** Leverage the power of AI to craft SEO-friendly content. Get keyword suggestions, readability scores, and meta-tag recommendations. Let the AI automatically generate or refine paragraphs, headings, and product descriptions to align perfectly with SEO best practices.
– **Schema Management:** Implement structured data with ease. Xagio SEO auto-generates schema markup for pages, products, FAQs, and more, increasing your chance of achieving rich results in search engines.
– **Rank Tracking:** Stay on top of your performance with accurate rank tracking. Monitor keyword positions, discover trends, and analyze SERP fluctuations for your target keywords. Track progress over time with detailed reports and visual insights.
– **Niche Hunter:** Uncover lucrative niches with our built-in Niche Hunter. Using AI-driven market analysis, quickly identify profitable topics, low-competition keywords, and emerging trends to stay ahead of the curve.
– **User-Friendly Interface:** Manage your entire SEO strategy, from AI-driven insights to niche discovery, through one straightforward dashboard that’s designed to streamline your workflow.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `xagio-seo` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Access the Xagio SEO dashboard from the WordPress admin area to begin configuring your SEO settings.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

**Q:** How do I get started with Xagio SEO after installation?
**A:** Once you activate Xagio SEO, navigate to the Xagio SEO dashboard in your WordPress admin area. There you will find initial setup prompts, including connecting your site to the Xagio Panel API (if you have a license), and configuring basic SEO settings. Follow the on-screen instructions to leverage the plugin’s AI-driven analysis and content optimization tools.

**Q:** Do I need any technical SEO knowledge to use Xagio SEO?
**A:** Not at all! Xagio SEO is designed to be beginner-friendly. Its AI-driven recommendations guide you step-by-step, helping you implement best practices even if you’re new to SEO. Advanced users can also delve deeper into settings for finer control.

**Q:** Is Xagio SEO compatible with other plugins and themes?
**A:** Yes. Xagio SEO is designed to work with most themes and plugins. If you run into compatibility issues, feel free to reach out to our support team.

**Q:** How often are updates released?
**A:** We release updates regularly, including feature enhancements, bug fixes, and security patches. You can easily update directly from your WordPress admin dashboard.
